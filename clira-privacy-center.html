Privacy Policy

1. Introduction and Our Privacy Philosophy

Clira – Cleaner is an iOS-only mobile application designed to assist users in organizing and optimizing their device storage by identifying duplicate or unnecessary photos, videos, contacts, and calendar events.

Our core architectural principle is privacy by design and by default. The App was intentionally developed so that personal data remains under the user’s direct control at all times.

We do not operate a cloud storage platform, we do not maintain user accounts, and we do not build behavioral or advertising profiles.

  • No account creation is required.
  • No personal information is requested from users.
  • No personal data is transmitted to our infrastructure.
  • No personal data is monetized.
  • No data is shared with advertisers.

2. Scope of Application

This Privacy Policy applies exclusively to:

  • The Clira – Cleaner iOS mobile application;
  • All features provided within the App;
  • Users located in the European Union, United States, Japan, and other regions where the App is available.

This Policy does not apply to Apple Inc., the Apple App Store, or device-level data processing conducted by the iOS operating system. Users should consult Apple’s own privacy policies for information about Apple ID accounts, billing systems, and device analytics.

3. No Personal Data Collection

Clira does not collect, store, process, transmit, distribute, sell, lease, or otherwise handle personal data outside of the user’s device.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names or surnames
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Postal addresses
  • IP addresses for profiling
  • Device identifiers for tracking
  • Location data
  • Biometric information
  • Usage analytics for marketing
  • Behavioral tracking data

Because we do not collect personal information, we do not maintain centralized databases containing user content.

4. Device Permissions and On-Device Processing

In order to perform its core functions, the App may request specific permissions from the user. These permissions enable analysis of data already stored on the user’s device.

All data processing is executed locally within the secure environment of iOS. No data is exported to external servers controlled by us.

4.1 Photo Library Access

The App may request access to your photo library to:

  • Detect duplicate images
  • Identify visually similar media
  • Detect large or space-consuming files
  • Assist in storage optimization

Image comparison algorithms operate exclusively on-device. Media files are not copied, uploaded, or mirrored externally.

4.2 Contacts Access

The App may request permission to analyze contact entries in order to:

  • Identify duplicate contacts
  • Detect incomplete or inconsistent entries
  • Assist users in manual cleanup

Contact data remains fully under user control and is never transmitted to us.

4.3 Calendar Access

The App may request access to calendar data to:

  • Detect duplicate events
  • Identify outdated calendar entries
  • Support user-initiated organization

Calendar information remains stored locally on the device and is not externally stored or processed.

5. Subscription and Payment Processing

Clira offers optional premium subscriptions, including trial periods. Subscription payments are processed exclusively by Apple via the App Store.

We do not:

  • Access payment card numbers
  • Store billing credentials
  • Handle financial transactions directly
  • Receive full payment details

Apple manages subscription billing, renewals, and refunds in accordance with Apple’s policies.

6. No Advertising and No Tracking

Clira does not integrate advertising networks or tracking SDKs.

  • No third-party advertising
  • No targeted ads
  • No cross-app tracking
  • No analytics-based behavioral profiling

The App does not monetize user data in any form.

7. International Legal Compliance

7.1 European Union – GDPR

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), personal data controllers are required to provide transparency regarding processing activities. Because Clira does not collect or process personal data externally, we do not operate as a data controller in relation to personal information handled within the App.

7.2 United States – CCPA / CPRA

We do not collect personal information as defined under California privacy laws. Therefore:

  • There is no personal information to disclose.
  • There is no personal information to delete.
  • No personal information is sold or shared.

7.3 Japan – APPI

Under Japan’s Act on the Protection of Personal Information (APPI), businesses must clarify acquisition and retention practices. Clira does not acquire or retain personal information on remote servers.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under any age threshold. Since we do not collect personal data from any users, there is no database containing minors’ information.

9. Security Considerations

Clira relies on Apple’s iOS security architecture. All processing occurs within the device sandbox environment.

Because we do not operate centralized data storage systems, risks associated with server breaches, unauthorized third-party database access, or remote data leaks are minimized.

Users remain responsible for managing device-level security settings.

10. User Rights

In jurisdictions that provide rights of access, correction, deletion, portability, or restriction of processing, such rights apply to personal data held by a controller. Since Clira does not collect or retain personal data, these rights do not apply to data stored solely on the user’s device.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in:

  • Legal requirements
  • Regulatory guidance
  • Application functionality

The “Last Updated” date at the top of this document will indicate when revisions were made.
